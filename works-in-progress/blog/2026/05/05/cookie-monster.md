author: Kyle Eggleston
date: Tuesday, May 2, 2026
---

So we bought some Cookie Monster Ice Cream. It's blue, like the Cookie Monster. Tastes like vanilla with chocolate
chips in it. I don't see the appeal of it though. I mean, if it just tastes like vanilla ice cream with chocolate 
chips, what's the point of it? It's good, don't get me wrong, but it just feels like normal ice cream.

Might as well buy chocolate chip cookie dough and be done with it. Ya know? Of course, you do! That's how we roll
at times. I mean come on now. If you're going to paint ice cream blue and call it something it really isn't ...
why? Just why?
