Hiram Page was one of the Eight Witnesses to the Book of Mormon. During a time, he claims to have received revelations
from God for the LDS Church.[^1] The method he was receiving these revelations was through a seer stone. The same way
Joseph Smith was receiving revelation.

This caused many of the members of the early church to follow Page. Joseph was troubled by this and prayed to God
about it after serious reflection. He received a revelation in the form of D&C 28 directed to Oliver Cowdery.
The reveltation sthated that there was only one mouthpiece for the church and that was Joseph Smith. It also stated
that Oliver was to speak with Hiram Page about it.

Page's revelations were false and not from God, they were from Satan.[^2] Hiram Page accepted that he was in the wrong
and repented.

I remember being in seminary and the teacher presenting this story. When he stated that there were some early saints
that believed in seer stones I thought he was crazy. Little did I know the real truth behind it all, that seer stones
were real and that Joseph did use them in both treasure hunting and receiving revelation. (Which is *still* a 
troublesome fact for me, but that's a topic for another day.)

[^1]: Doctrine and Covenants 28
[^2]: Doctrine and Covenants 28:11
