Madison Park woke up as her alarm went off. "The time is oh six hundred hours." The computer said. Madison groaned. Why
did morning have to come so early? Getting out of bed, she got dressed for the day. Hopefully today would be a 
different day than the last month had given her. Looking out her bedroom window, Madison looked at Shuka below. She
envied the people of Shuka. They didn't have a station to upkeep. Their lives were probably easier than hers was.

"Computer, what does my schedule look like today?" Madison called out into the air.

The computer made a beeping noise before responding. "Mandatory staff meeting at oh eight hundred. dockworkers staff
meeting at oh nine hundred. Maintenance due on shuttles three, five, eleven, and fourteen. End of schedule."

Madison sighed. She nodded her head. "Right. Meetings and then work. Makes sense for a typical Monday." She was
tempted to cancel the meetings, but that wouldn't be possible. Important things needed to be discussed.
